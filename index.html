it got better-

the transitions in life
it put us all at ease.
the paths seem somewhat bright
realities from our dreams.

then something big happened
in year twenty nineteen,
we're having a hard time
with life in quarantine.

preventing contractions,
and now sheltered-in-place;
year twenty twenty's start
felt like it went to waste.

but that's negativity
and that's not what we need,
there's more to being safe
than not leaving as pleased.

right, it's overwhelming,
but continue to hope.
continue to uplift,
that's a good way to cope.

it is temporary.
it was better before.
once again we will wait,
for reality once more.

-it gets better.
